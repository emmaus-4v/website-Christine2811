Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈)

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment. The group currently consists of Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Originally a nine-piece group, member Woojin left Stray Kids due to undisclosed personal reasons in October 2019. The group released their extended play 'Mixtape' in January 2018 and officially debuted on March 25 with I Am Not. Stray Kids was created through the survival program with the same name, Stray Kids. Stray Kids debuted on March 25, 2018.
