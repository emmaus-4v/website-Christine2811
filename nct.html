NCT (엔시티)

NCT, an acronym for ‘Neo Culture Technology’, is a new boy group concept. They are described as having an unlimited number of members. The group currently has 23 members and consists of Taeyong, Taeil, Johnny, Yuta, Kun, Doyoung, Ten, Jaehyun, WinWin, Jungwoo, Lucas, Mark, Xiaojun, Hendery, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, YangYang, Shotaro, Sungchan, Chenle, and Jisung. NCT promotes in different sub-units. The current sub-units are: 'NCT U', 'NCT 127', 'NCT Dream' and 'WayV'. They are under SM Entertainment. Their fandom name is called 'NCTzen' (meaning all the fans are citizens of NCT).
